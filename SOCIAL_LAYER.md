# Bird Game 3 – Social Systems v1.0

---

## 1. Philosophy

Social should:

- Be optional
- Enhance chaos
- Never block solo enjoyment
- Never introduce grief traps

Multiplayer is ambient, not required.

---

## 2. Player Visibility

### Always Visible

- Bird model
- Player name (toggleable)
- Level badge
- Trail cosmetic

### Conditional Visibility

- Wanted icon (Heat ≥ threshold)
- Grounded animation

---

## 3. Player Interaction Rules

### PvP

- Enabled everywhere except Sanctuary
- No team damage system
- No factions in v1.0

### Soft Proximity Awareness

- Nearby player counter (top UI)
- Mini-map dots for Wanted players only

---

## 4. Emotes (Cosmetic Only)

Quick emote wheel:

- Squawk
- Flap taunt
- Victory spin
- Mock salute

No gameplay impact.

---

## 5. Social Stats

Profile displays:

| Stat |
|---|
| Total NPCs hit |
| Total players hit |
| Times grounded |
| Longest streak |
| Total coins earned |
| Total distance flown |

---

## 6. Future Hooks (Not v1.0)

- Flocks (guilds)
- Limited-time city events
- Territory control

These are documented but **not implemented in v1.0**.
